Visit Abba Jifar Palace

Abba Jifar Palace is located in Jimma, a historic city in southwestern Ethiopia. The palace sits on Jiren Hill, offering visitors a peaceful environment, beautiful scenery, and an unforgettable historical experience.

Location

Jimma, Oromia Region, Ethiopia

Opening Hours

Open daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Opening hours may vary during holidays and special events.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is during the morning or in the dry season when the weather is cool, pleasant, and suitable for exploring.

What to Expect

  • Explore the historic palace and surrounding buildings
  • Learn about the history of the Jimma Kingdom
  • Enjoy scenic views from Jiren Hill
  • Experience local culture and heritage

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Bring water and sun protection
  • Respect local culture and historical sites
